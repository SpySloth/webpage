Medical Robots

The main type of medical robots is the surgical robot which are used for preforming surgery. There are also different types of medical robots all used for differnet reasons. These include rehabilitation robots, biorobots, telepresence robots and pharmacy automation.

One of the most know medical robots is the Da Vinci

The Da Vinci surgical robot is used for preforming surgary on people, the main usage of them in surgary is for small operation.
