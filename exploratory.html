Exploratory Robots

What are Exploratory Robots?

Exploration Robotics is all about sending mobile robots into natural or artificial environments where people can’t go (or don’t want to go) in order to gather valuable sensory data and, sometimes, perform physical tasks.

Curiosity Rover

Curiosity was a mars rover sent on the 26 November of 2011, it's main goal was to explore the Gale crater. It was originaly lanched to survive on Mars for 2 year but it is still going and exploring the surface of Mars.
